Bio

Hi! My name is Christine Doig and I'm a Master Student in Innovation and Research in Informatics at FIB-UPC, Barcelona, Spain. I'm specializing in Data Mining and Business Intelligence and I'm interested in Data Science, Machine Learning, Visualization, R, Python and Open Source.

I was born in Austin, Texas, but grew up in Barcelona, Spain. I studied Industrial Engineering at the Polytechnic Univeristy of Catalonia in Barcelona. I wrote my Master Thesis at the Institute for Automation of Complex Power Systems at the E.ON Energy Research Center at RWTH Aachen University in Germany. I have worked as a Process Engineer at Procter&Gamble and as a Business analyst for the Spanish Management Consulting Firm, Bluecap.

When I'm not computering, I enjoy watching indie films and travelling.

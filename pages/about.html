About

I've started this blog to share my personal journey to Data Science. I've just started a Master's Degree in Innovation and Research in Informatics, with a specialization in Data Mining and Business Intelligence. I want to organize my findings and thoughts to help myself in the learning process and gather my personal project portfolio in one place.

For the last couple of months, I've been researching and learning a lot online. There are so many materials, courses, information on Data Science available, that it can be time consuming and hard to navigate. Sometimes it can be overwhelming and discouraging when learning on your own. After going through so many resources, I felt I could share the information I've gathered in my journey to help other self-taught Data Scientist. I'm not a professor or an expert, just someone passionate about learning and willing to share my findings. I do have an Engineering background and some Business analyst experiece, but I'll try to provide a very introductory path, so anyone can join me in this exciting journey.

For this blog I've decided to classify the posts in the following categories:

  • Programming
  • Databases
  • Statistics
  • Machine Learning
  • Visualization
  • Big data

Tools that I'm going to talk about in this blog:

  • Python
  • JavaScript
  • D3.js
  • Unix command line
  • Git
  • SQL
  • Hadoop, MapReduce
  • R
  • SAS
  • Clojure

Thanks for reading! I hope you find my blog helpful and useful in your path to becoming a Data Scientist!
